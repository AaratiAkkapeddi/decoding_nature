(De)coding Nature

This visual arts course explores the intersection between natural patterns and computational thinking by combining outdoor observation with creative coding. Students ventured into Prospect Park and the Brooklyn Botanic Garden to study plant/animal life like flocking birds and branching trees. We then returned to the classroom to recreate these patterns using Javascript.
